Earthquake killed 144 persons in Turkiye, 2025.

The year 2025 was marked by a seismic event of great magnitude,
which generated concern on a global scale. A strong earthquake struck the region of Myanmar,
a country neighboring Turkey, causing destruction and mobilizing humanitarian aid efforts. Although the epicenter was located in Myanmar, its proximity to Turkey, a country that unfortunately
already has a recent history of significant seismic activity, has further raised the level of alert and international commotion.
Authorities in both countries are working to assess the extent of the damage, the number of victims and the most urgent needs of the affected situations.
